Welcome to Seahshore

Seashore Group is one of Qatar’s largest and most dynamic multidisciplinary business conglomerates, headquartered in Al Khor, about 50 km north of Doha. Established in 1989 by Chairman Mr. Saeed Salem Al Mohannadi, a prominent Qatari businessman, and Managing Director Mr. Mohamed Ali, an accomplished Indian entrepreneur, the Group has grown from just three workers to a thriving enterprise with over 3,500 employees and assets exceeding QR 1 billion. Today, Seashore Group comprises 15 specialized divisions, operated by expert managers, engineers, technicians, and skilled tradesmen, all supported by state-of-the-art factories, machinery, laboratories, and full-service staff facilities. As an ISO 9001:2008 certified company, we are committed to delivering high-quality services and solutions that meet international standards, proudly serving both national and global clients across diverse sectors.

Our FAQ’S

Why i should choose your company?

With over three decades of experience, Seashore Group has earned a reputation for reliability, innovation, and excellence in Qatar’s industrial and commercial sectors

Is Seashore Group certified to meet international quality standards?

Yes, we are an ISO 9001:2008 certified company. We adhere strictly to international standards of quality, safety, and environmental responsibility in all our operations and services.

What services does Seashore Group offer?

Seashore Group is a multidisciplinary enterprise with 15 specialized divisions offering services in industrial contracting, engineering, metal works, waste management, transportation, marine services, and more. We provide end-to-end solutions tailored to the needs of various industries across Qatar and beyond.

How experienced is your team?

We have over 3,500 professionals on our team — including expert engineers, technicians, project managers, and support staff. Many of our divisions are led by industry veterans with decades of hands-on experience, ensuring every project is executed with precision.
